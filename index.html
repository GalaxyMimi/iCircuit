Since the humanity has started to polute the planet Earth, our future existence has got in danger. But sometimes it's hard to do big decisions, so we started our company iCircuit, to give you a possibility to make a small but very important step in the right direction.

About iCircuit

  • We try to produce and sell energy saving devices as convenient as possible.

  • Also, we make presentations in schools and companies to clarify about how to save energy.

  • We want to make renewable energy a trend.

  • Our solar powered #WorldSavers merch was designed - amongst other things - to show young people that the energy saving lifestyle is a cool thing.

  • Throgh our donation button you get a possibility to support us by making our product cheap and buyable for everyone.

  • We also cooperate with some companies in Germany which support us with some money.
